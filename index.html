    Quality Algorithms

    Building better technology always wins We founded the company on one simple premise: “building better technology always wins and cutting-corners always loses”. In order to be successful, customer expectations must be delivered without ever compromising on high quality standards.

    Delivering on customer expectations High quality implies that our code is well tested and bug free, respects web development standards (W3C Standards), respects web security standards (NIST), is able to scale to serve millions of users, is deployed in a cost-effective manner that aims to maximize customer profits, and ultimately delivers on customer expectations.

    Agile Methodology We believe that client feedback is crucial in delivering an impeccable product. We aim to engage our client throughout the product lifecycle. We work in sprints and follow an agile methodology consisting of 5 sequential phases:

  • Project Initiation
  • Requirements Gathering and System Design
  • Development
  • Quality Assurance and Testing
  • Deployment and Support
